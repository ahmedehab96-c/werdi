# Privacy Policy — Werdi (وردي)

**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

Werdi is a Quran memorization and reading app. We respect your privacy.

## Data we collect

- **Account data (optional):** If you sign in with Supabase, your email and profile name are stored on our Supabase project to sync bookmarks and progress.
- **Local data:** Memorization progress, review items, reading preferences, and reminders are stored on your device.
- **Microphone (Tasmee3):** Used only for on-device speech recognition during recitation tests. Audio is not uploaded to our servers.

## Data we do not collect

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not record or store your voice on our servers.

## Third-party services

- **Supabase** — authentication and cloud sync (when signed in).
- **MP3Quran / Islamic Network CDN** — Quran audio streaming.
- **Google Fonts** — typography (Amiri, Tajawal).

## Your choices

- Use the app as a guest without creating an account.
- Disable notifications in system settings.
- Revoke microphone permission in system settings.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository or contact the developer through the store listing.
