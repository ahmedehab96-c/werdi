## Short Description (Play)

## Full Description

Werdi is a Quran memorization and revision companion designed for daily consistency.

Key features:
- Memorization sessions by surah and ayah range.
- Smart review cards to reinforce weak areas.
- Self-test (Tasmee3) with instant grading and history.
- Quran browsing by surah and juz, with bookmarks.
- Tafsir and translation preview.
- Audio recitation controls.
- Daily goals, streaks, and achievements.
- Arabic-first experience with full English support.
- Light and dark mode support.
